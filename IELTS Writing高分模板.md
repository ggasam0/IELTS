# 《IELTS General Training Writing 高分模板》

## 适用说明

这份文件专门对应 IELTS General Training 的 Writing 部分：

* Task 1：Letter
* Task 2：Essay

它不是评分标准总结，而是“可直接写”的模板文件。

适合以下情况：

* 你知道题型，但写起来没有结构
* 你总是卡在开头句和段落组织
* 你能想到内容，但语言不稳定
* 你想先把框架写稳，再慢慢打磨语言

---

## 一、General Writing 的稳定思路

写作提分最稳的顺序不是先追求复杂句，而是：

1. 先保证结构正确
2. 再保证句子完整
3. 再保证逻辑自然
4. 最后再优化词汇和句式

所以不管是 Task 1 还是 Task 2，最重要的是：

* 每段功能明确
* 每句都在完成任务
* 不写空话
* 不偏题

---

## 二、Task 1 Letter：三类信件模板

General Training Task 1 最稳的分法是：

* Formal letter
* Semi-formal letter
* Informal letter

---

## 1. Formal Letter

### 常见题型

* complain
* request information
* apply
* report a problem
* ask for action

### 稳定结构

* Opening: 说明写信目的
* Body 1: 交代背景
* Body 2: 说明要求 / 问题 / 希望对方怎么做
* Closing: 礼貌收尾

### 开头模板

* I am writing to express my dissatisfaction with ...
* I am writing to enquire about ...
* I am writing regarding ...
* I am writing to request ...
* I am writing in connection with ...

### 正文模板

* The reason for my letter is that ...
* I would like to explain the situation in detail.
* Unfortunately, ...
* This caused considerable inconvenience because ...
* I would therefore appreciate it if you could ...
* I would be grateful if you could ...
* I would also like to request that ...

### 结尾模板

* I look forward to your prompt response.
* I would appreciate your attention to this matter.
* I hope the issue can be resolved as soon as possible.
* Thank you for your time and consideration.

### Formal 完整骨架

```text
Dear Sir or Madam,

I am writing to ...

The reason for my letter is that ...

Unfortunately, ...

As a result, ...

I would therefore appreciate it if you could ...

I look forward to your prompt response.

Yours faithfully,
[Name]
```

---

## 2. Semi-formal Letter

### 常见题型

* write to a manager
* write to a teacher
* write to a landlord
* write to a neighbour
* explain a situation politely

### 稳定结构

* Opening: 说明写信原因
* Body 1: 背景和情况
* Body 2: 请求 / 建议 / 解释
* Closing: 礼貌而自然

### 开头模板

* I am writing to let you know that ...
* I am writing to explain ...
* I am writing about ...
* I am writing to ask whether ...

### 正文模板

* As you may know, ...
* The main reason is that ...
* Because of this, ...
* I was wondering whether it would be possible to ...
* I would really appreciate your help with ...
* Please let me know if ...

### 结尾模板

* I look forward to hearing from you.
* Thank you in advance for your help.
* I appreciate your understanding.
* Please let me know what you think.

### Semi-formal 完整骨架

```text
Dear Mr Smith / Dear Ms Brown,

I am writing to ...

As you may know, ...

The problem is that ...

Because of this, I was wondering whether ...

I would really appreciate your help.

I look forward to hearing from you.

Best regards,
[Name]
```

---

## 3. Informal Letter

### 常见题型

* invite
* thank
* apologise
* give news
* make suggestions

### 稳定结构

* Opening: 自然打招呼
* Body 1: 解释写信原因
* Body 2: 提供细节
* Body 3: 建议 / 邀请 / 道歉 / 收尾

### 开头模板

* I hope you’re doing well.
* It was great to hear from you.
* I’m writing to tell you about ...
* I’m writing because ...

### 正文模板

* I thought you’d like to know that ...
* The best thing about it is ...
* I’m really sorry that ...
* If you’re free, you should definitely ...
* Let me know if ...

### 结尾模板

* Hope to hear from you soon.
* Write back when you have time.
* I’m really looking forward to seeing you.
* Take care and stay in touch.

### Informal 完整骨架

```text
Dear [Name],

I hope you’re doing well.

I’m writing because ...

The reason is that ...

I thought you’d like to know ...

If you’re free, ...

Hope to hear from you soon.

Best,
[Name]
```

## 四、Task 1 最容易失分的点

### 1. 语气不对

Formal letter 却写得太像朋友聊天，会直接影响语域。

### 2. 漏掉 bullet points

题目给的三个 bullet 必须全部覆盖。

### 3. 背景太长

开头写太多废话，会挤掉真正要写的要求和动作。

### 4. 没有明确 request / action

特别是投诉信，如果只说问题，不说希望对方怎么做，任务完成度会下降。

### 5. 结尾太突然

最好始终有一句 polite closing。

## 五、Task 2 Essay：五类高频模板

General Training Task 2 和 Academic Task 2 在结构上几乎可以共用。

最常见的 5 类：

* agree or disagree
* discuss both views
* advantages and disadvantages
* problem and solution
* direct questions

---

## 1. Agree or Disagree

### 稳定结构

* Introduction
* Body 1: 主观点 1
* Body 2: 主观点 2
* Conclusion

### 开头模板

* Some people believe that ..., while others disagree.
* It is often argued that ...
* I completely agree that ...
* I partly agree with this view because ...

### Body 段模板

* The first reason is that ...
* This is mainly because ...
* For example, ...
* As a result, ...

### 结尾模板

* In conclusion, I believe that ...
* Overall, I would argue that ...

### 完整骨架

```text
It is often argued that ...

I agree / partly agree with this view.

The first reason is that ...
For example, ...

Another important point is that ...
This means ...

In conclusion, I believe that ...
```

---

## 2. Discuss Both Views

### 稳定结构

* Introduction
* Body 1: View A
* Body 2: View B + your opinion
* Conclusion

### 开头模板

* People have different views about whether ...
* While some people argue that ..., others believe that ...

### Body 段模板

* On the one hand, ...
* Supporters of this view often argue that ...
* On the other hand, ...
* In my opinion, ...

### 结尾模板

* In conclusion, although both views have merit, I believe that ...

### 完整骨架

```text
People have different views about whether ...

On the one hand, some people believe that ...
This is because ...
For example, ...

On the other hand, others argue that ...
I find this view more convincing because ...

In conclusion, although both sides make valid points, I believe that ...
```

---

## 3. Advantages and Disadvantages

### 稳定结构

* Introduction
* Body 1: advantages
* Body 2: disadvantages
* Conclusion

### 开头模板

* In recent years, it has become increasingly common to ...
* This trend has both benefits and drawbacks.

### Body 段模板

* One major advantage is that ...
* Another benefit is ...
* However, one clear disadvantage is that ...
* A further drawback is ...

### 结尾模板

* Overall, I believe the advantages outweigh the disadvantages.
* Overall, I believe the disadvantages are more significant.

---

## 4. Problem and Solution

### 稳定结构

* Introduction
* Body 1: problems
* Body 2: solutions
* Conclusion

### 开头模板

* One serious issue in modern society is ...
* This problem can be seen in many countries today.

### Body 段模板

* One major problem is that ...
* This can lead to ...
* A practical solution would be to ...
* Another effective measure is ...

### 结尾模板

* In conclusion, although this is a serious issue, it can be addressed through ...

---

## 5. Direct Questions Essay

### 稳定结构

* Introduction
* Body 1: answer question 1
* Body 2: answer question 2
* Conclusion

### 开头模板

* This essay will discuss the main reasons for this trend and suggest possible responses.
* This essay will explain why this happens and whether it is a positive development.

### Body 段模板

* The main reason is that ...
* Another contributing factor is ...
* In my view, the best response would be ...

### 结尾模板

* In conclusion, this trend is largely driven by ..., and it can best be addressed by ...

## 九、Writing 最容易失分的点

### 1. 句子短但散

不是不会写，而是每句之间没逻辑。

### 2. 只写观点，不写解释

Task 2 特别怕“一句观点 + 一句空例子”。

### 3. 例子太假或太空

不需要编很夸张的例子，写普通而合理的例子就够。

### 4. 模板痕迹太重

模板是骨架，不是全文。写的时候必须替换成题目自己的内容。

### 5. Task 1 语气乱

信件题的语气错误会非常明显。

---

## 十、推荐练法

### Task 1

* 每次只练一种语气
* 先写开头和结尾
* 再补正文两段

### Task 2

* 先只写提纲
* 再写 introduction + 一个 body
* 最后再写完整篇

### 最稳的训练顺序

1. 先背开头句和连接句
2. 再背 5 类题型骨架
3. 再按题目替换关键词
4. 最后限时写

---

## 十一、最后提醒

写作提分最常见的误区是：

* 一上来追求高级词
* 一上来写复杂句
* 一上来就完整限时写

更高效的方式是：

* 先把结构写对
* 再把段落写稳
* 再追求语言升级

如果你继续往下补资料，下一步最自然的是：

* `Task 1 信件题库版`
* `Task 2 议论文题库版`
* `7 分范文拆解版`

这样 IELTS 的听、说、写三块就会更完整。
