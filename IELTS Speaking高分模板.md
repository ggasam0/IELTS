## 一、7 分答案的核心标准

口语 7 分并不要求你说得像演讲者，而是要求你做到下面几点：

* 回答切题，不跑偏
* 有结构，不是想到哪说到哪
* 句子自然，不全是碎片
* 有细节，不只会讲空话
* 能展开观点，而不是一句话结束
* 偶尔停顿没关系，但不能频繁卡死

所以 Speaking 的关键不是“背最难的词”，而是：

* 先把结构稳定下来
* 再把内容具体起来
* 最后让表达自然一点

## 二、Part 2 的稳定结构

Part 2 最稳的做法不是硬背整篇，而是记住一个 `6 步结构`。

### 结构总览

1. 直接点题
2. 交代背景
3. 描述具体细节
4. 加一个小故事或小画面
5. 解释为什么重要
6. 用一句总结自然收尾

### 通用骨架

```text
I’d like to talk about ...

This happened / I got it / I experienced it ...

What I remember most is ...

One detail that stands out is ...

The reason it was important to me is that ...

So overall, I’d say it was a really meaningful experience / person / place for me.
```

### 稳定开头句

* I’d like to talk about a person who has had a strong influence on me.
* I’d like to describe a place that I really enjoy visiting.
* I’m going to talk about an experience that was memorable for me.
* I’d like to describe something I bought that turned out to be very useful.
* One thing that immediately comes to mind is ...

### 背景句

* I first came across this when I was ...
* This happened a few years ago when ...
* I got to know this person through ...
* I visited this place during ...
* I bought it because ...

### 细节展开句

* What I liked most was ...
* One thing that stood out was ...
* What made it different was ...
* I still remember that ...
* Another detail worth mentioning is ...

### 解释意义句

* It mattered to me because ...
* I found it useful mainly because ...
* The reason I still remember it is that ...
* It changed the way I think about ...
* It made me realise that ...

### 收尾句

* So overall, it was a very positive experience for me.
* Looking back, I think it was more important than I realised at the time.
* That’s why I still remember it so clearly.
* So yes, I’d definitely say it was a meaningful part of my life.

## 三、Part 2 备考卡片法

拿到题卡后，1 分钟准备时间不要写整句，直接写 4 格信息：

### 1. Who / What

这个人 / 地方 / 物品 / 事件是什么

### 2. When / Where

什么时候，在哪里

### 3. Details

两个到三个能展开的小细节

### 4. Why

为什么重要、为什么喜欢、为什么难忘

### 示例

题目：Describe a useful thing you bought.

准备笔记可以写成：

```text
item: laptop
when: last year
details: light / fast / for work and English study
why: saves time / very practical / use every day
```

这样你回答时就能自然展开，不需要背整段。

## 四、Part 2 卡住时的补救句

如果你突然卡住，不要停死，可以用这些句子续上。

### 争取时间

* Let me think about how to explain that more clearly.
* There are actually a couple of reasons for that.
* It’s a bit hard to describe, but I’ll try my best.
* What I mean is that ...
* To put it another way ...

### 补细节

* For example, ...
* In particular, ...
* One small detail I still remember is ...
* Another thing I should mention is ...
* What made it more interesting was ...

### 补感受

* At the time, I felt ...
* Looking back, I think ...
* To be honest, I didn’t expect it to ...
* What surprised me most was ...
* I didn’t realise it then, but ...

### 拉回主题

* So that’s basically why this experience stayed with me.
* And that’s the main reason I chose this topic.
* So overall, that’s what made it memorable.

## 1. Describe a Person

### 可覆盖题型

* a person you admire
* a helpful person
* someone you enjoy talking to
* someone who taught you something

### 模板

```text
I’d like to talk about a person I really admire, and that person is ...

I got to know him / her when ...

What I appreciate most is that ...

He / She once ...

That experience left a strong impression on me because ...

So overall, I’d say this person has influenced me in a very positive way.
```

### 高分补充句

* He isn’t the loudest person in the room, but he is extremely dependable.
* What I respect most is his ability to stay calm under pressure.
* I learned a lot from the way he dealt with everyday situations.

## 六、Part 3 的稳定展开法

Part 3 最容易丢分的原因不是“没观点”，而是：

* 只回答一句
* 只给结论不给原因
* 没有例子
* 说到一半自己绕乱了

最稳的做法是记住一个 `4 步展开法`：

1. 先给观点
2. 解释原因
3. 举一个例子
4. 做一个对比或补充

### 通用骨架

```text
I think ...

The main reason is that ...

For example, ...

At the same time, ...

So overall, ...
```

### 示例

问题：Do people spend too much time on phones?

```text
Yes, I think many people probably do.

The main reason is that phones are now used for almost everything, including work, social life, and entertainment.

For example, even when people have only a short break, they often check messages or social media automatically.

At the same time, I don’t think phones are the problem by themselves. The real issue is how people manage their habits.

So overall, I’d say phones are useful, but many people do rely on them too much.
```

## 七、Part 3 六大高频题型模板

---

## 1. 原因题

### 问法

* Why do people ...
* Why is this popular ...
* Why has this changed ...

### 模板

* I think there are a few reasons for that.
* The main one is that ...
* Another reason is that ...
* For instance, ...
* So it’s not surprising that ...

### 例句

* I think there are a few reasons for that.
* The main one is that modern life is much faster than before.
* Another reason is that people now have more access to technology.
* So it makes sense that their habits have changed.

---

## 2. 利弊题

### 问法

* What are the advantages and disadvantages of ...
* Is this a positive or negative development ...

### 模板

* I think it has both benefits and drawbacks.
* On the positive side, ...
* However, the downside is that ...
* So whether it is good or bad depends on ...

### 例句

* I think it has both benefits and drawbacks.
* On the positive side, it saves time and makes communication easier.
* However, the downside is that people may become too dependent on it.
* So whether it is positive or negative depends on how it is used.

---

## 3. 过去与现在对比题

### 问法

* Are things different now from the past ...
* How has ... changed over time ...

### 模板

* Yes, I think there has been a clear change.
* In the past, ...
* These days, ...
* One major difference is ...
* I think this change happened because ...

### 例句

* Yes, I think there has been a clear change.
* In the past, people relied more on face-to-face communication.
* These days, a lot of interaction happens online.
* One major difference is the speed of communication.
* I think this change happened mainly because technology became part of daily life.

---

## 4. 比较两类人题

### 问法

* Do young people and older people think differently about ...
* Who is more likely to ...

### 模板

* I think they often see it differently.
* Younger people tend to ...
* Older people, by contrast, are more likely to ...
* Of course, this is only a general trend.
* It usually depends on personality and background as well.

### 例句

* I think they often see it differently.
* Younger people tend to adapt to new technology more quickly.
* Older people, by contrast, may prefer more familiar ways of doing things.
* Of course, this is only a general trend.
* It also depends on education and personal interest.

---

## 5. 政府 / 学校 / 个人责任题

### 问法

* Should the government ...
* Is it the responsibility of schools ...
* What should individuals do ...

### 模板

* I think responsibility should be shared.
* Governments can ...
* At the same time, individuals should ...
* Schools or families also play a role because ...
* So I don’t think one side can solve it alone.

### 例句

* I think responsibility should be shared.
* Governments can provide better public systems and clearer rules.
* At the same time, individuals should also change their daily behaviour.
* Schools and families matter as well because habits start early.
* So I don’t think one side can solve the issue alone.

## 6. 未来预测题

### 问法

* How will this change in the future ...
* What might happen in the next few years ...

### 模板

* I think this will probably continue to change.
* In the future, ...
* This is mainly because ...
* However, I don’t think the change will be complete.
* Some traditional aspects will probably remain.

### 例句

* I think this will probably continue to change.
* In the future, more services are likely to become digital.
* This is mainly because people value convenience and speed.
* However, I don’t think the change will be complete.
* Some face-to-face interaction will still remain important.

## 九、口语里最安全的“7 分词汇”

不需要故意堆难词，先把这些稳定表达用熟：

* practical
* convenient
* reliable
* meaningful
* memorable
* comfortable
* flexible
* efficient
* stressful
* beneficial
* balanced
* realistic
* gradually
* obviously
* in the long run
* to some extent
* in my experience
* at the same time
* one thing that stands out
* what matters most is

这些词的特点是：

* 不夸张
* 适合大多数题目
* 很容易和例子、原因、感受搭配

## 十、最后提醒

Speaking 提分最稳的顺序是：

1. 先背结构，不先背整篇
2. 先会展开，再追求高级词
3. 先说具体，再说抽象
4. 先说自然，再说漂亮
