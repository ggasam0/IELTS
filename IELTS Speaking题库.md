# 《IELTS Speaking 题库》

## 适用说明

这份题库用于配合以下资料一起使用：

* [IELTS General手册.md](c:/Users/Administrator/Desktop/IELTS/IELTS%20General手册.md)
* [IELTS Speaking高分模板.md](c:/Users/Administrator/Desktop/IELTS/IELTS%20Speaking高分模板.md)
* [IELTS 30天打卡计划.md](c:/Users/Administrator/Desktop/IELTS/IELTS%2030天打卡计划.md)

用途很直接：

* `Part 1` 快速抽题
* `Part 2` 题卡训练
* `Part 3` 观点展开训练
* 整套口语模拟

---

## 使用方法

### 方式 1：Part 1 快练

* 任意选 2 个 Part 1 话题
* 每个话题回答 3 到 4 题
* 每题控制在 `20-30 秒`

### 方式 2：Part 2 单题练习

* 从 Part 2 随机抽 1 张题卡
* 准备 `1 分钟`
* 回答 `1.5-2 分钟`

### 方式 3：Part 3 深度展开

* 针对同主题做 4 到 6 个 Part 3 问题
* 每题至少说 `4 句`
* 必须包括：观点 + 原因 + 例子 + 补充

### 方式 4：整套模拟

* Part 1：4 到 6 分钟
* Part 2：1 题
* Part 3：4 到 6 题
* 全程录音

## 一、Part 1 题库

---

## 1. Hometown

1. What kind of place is your hometown?
2. What do you like most about your hometown?
3. Has your hometown changed much in recent years?
4. Is your hometown a good place for young people?
5. Would you like to live there in the future?

---

## 2. Home and Accommodation

1. Do you live in a house or a flat?
2. What do you like about your home?
3. Which room do you spend the most time in?
4. Would you like to move to a different place in the future?
5. What would your ideal home be like?

---

## 3. Neighbourhood

1. Do you like the area where you live?
2. Is your neighbourhood quiet or noisy?
3. Are there enough facilities near your home?
4. How often do you go out in your neighbourhood?
5. Has your neighbourhood changed over time?

---

## 4. Work or Study

1. Do you work or are you a student?
2. What do you enjoy about your work or studies?
3. What part do you find most challenging?
4. Would you like to change anything about it?
5. Do you think your work or studies will be useful in the future?

---

## 5. Daily Routine

1. What is your daily routine like?
2. Do you prefer mornings or evenings?
3. Is your routine usually the same every day?
4. What part of the day do you enjoy most?
5. Would you like to change your routine in any way?

---

## 6. Free Time

1. What do you usually do in your free time?
2. Do you prefer spending free time alone or with others?
3. Do you have enough free time at the moment?
4. What do you do to relax?
5. Has the way you spend your free time changed over the years?

---

## 7. Hobbies

1. Do you have any hobbies?
2. How long have you had this hobby?
3. Why do you enjoy it?
4. Do you prefer hobbies that are active or relaxing?
5. Would you like to try a new hobby in the future?

---

## 8. Reading

1. Do you like reading?
2. What kind of books or articles do you usually read?
3. Did you read a lot as a child?
4. Do you prefer printed books or digital reading?
5. Do you think reading is important?

---

## 9. Music

1. Do you enjoy listening to music?
2. What kind of music do you like?
3. When do you usually listen to music?
4. Has your taste in music changed?
5. Do you think music is important in daily life?

---

## 10. Food

1. What kind of food do you usually eat?
2. Do you enjoy cooking?
3. Is there any food you particularly like?
4. Do you prefer eating at home or eating out?
5. Have your eating habits changed recently?

---

## 11. Shopping

1. Do you enjoy shopping?
2. What kind of things do you usually buy?
3. Do you prefer shopping online or in stores?
4. Do you often buy things for yourself?
5. Is shopping an enjoyable activity for you?

---

## 12. Transport

1. How do you usually travel around your city?
2. Do you often use public transport?
3. Is the transport system good where you live?
4. Do you prefer walking or using transport?
5. How important is transport in daily life?

---

## 13. Friends

1. Do you spend a lot of time with your friends?
2. What do you usually do together?
3. Do you prefer a few close friends or many friends?
4. Do you often make new friends?
5. What makes someone a good friend?

---

## 14. Weekends

1. What do you usually do on weekends?
2. Do you prefer busy weekends or quiet weekends?
3. Did you spend weekends differently in the past?
4. What is your ideal weekend like?
5. Do you think weekends are important?

---

## 15. Technology

1. Do you use technology a lot in daily life?
2. What device do you use most often?
3. Has technology made your life easier?
4. Is there any technology you find stressful?
5. Do you think people depend too much on technology?

---

## 16. Learning English

1. How long have you been learning English?
2. What part of English do you find most difficult?
3. Do you prefer speaking, listening, reading, or writing practice?
4. How do you usually practise English?
5. Why do you want to improve your English?

## 三、Part 3 题库

---

## 1. People and Influence

1. What kinds of people have the strongest influence on young people?
2. Do people learn more from family members or from teachers?
3. Why do some people become role models?
4. Do people today admire different kinds of people from the past?
5. Is it important for children to have someone to look up to?

---

## 2. Friendship and Social Life

1. How are friendships different today compared with the past?
2. Do adults make friends in the same way that children do?
3. Is it easier to maintain friendships now because of technology?
4. Why do some friendships last for a long time?
5. Do people need close friends to be happy?

---

## 3. Places and Cities

1. What makes a city a good place to live?
2. Why do some people prefer living in cities while others prefer smaller towns?
3. How can public places improve people’s quality of life?
4. Do you think cities are becoming better places to live?
5. How important are parks and public spaces in modern cities?

---

## 4. Travel

1. Why do people like travelling?
2. Is travelling more for relaxation or for learning?
3. Do people learn more from travelling abroad or from travelling in their own country?
4. How has travel changed in recent years?
5. Do you think travelling will become more common in the future?

---

## 5. Objects and Consumer Habits

1. Why do people buy things they do not really need?
2. Do expensive things always bring more satisfaction?
3. Why do some people keep objects for a long time?
4. Is it better to buy practical things or enjoyable things?
5. How have shopping habits changed because of technology?

---

## 6. Events and Celebrations

1. Why are celebrations important in society?
2. Are public celebrations better than private ones?
3. How have celebrations changed over time?
4. Do people spend too much money on celebrations?
5. What makes an event memorable?

---

## 7. Education

1. What makes someone a good teacher?
2. Are practical skills more important than theoretical knowledge?
3. How has education changed in recent years?
4. Should schools focus more on academic subjects or life skills?
5. Do you think students learn better alone or in groups?

---

## 8. Work

1. What qualities make someone good at their job?
2. How have people’s attitudes to work changed?
3. Do young people and older people have different expectations of work?
4. Is job satisfaction more important than salary?
5. What kinds of work may change most in the future?

---

## 9. Technology

1. What are the main advantages of technology in daily life?
2. Do you think people rely too much on technology today?
3. How has technology changed communication?
4. Should children use technology from a young age?
5. What kinds of technology will become more important in the future?

---

## 10. Environment

1. Why are environmental problems difficult to solve?
2. Should environmental protection be the responsibility of governments or individuals?
3. How can ordinary people reduce waste in daily life?
4. Do you think people are more environmentally aware than before?
5. What environmental changes may happen in the future?

---

## 11. Health and Lifestyle

1. Why do some people live unhealthy lifestyles even when they know the risks?
2. Is it easier or harder to stay healthy today than in the past?
3. What can schools do to help children develop healthy habits?
4. Do you think modern life is too stressful?
5. How important is exercise in daily life?

---

## 12. Leisure and Hobbies

1. Why do people need hobbies?
2. Are hobbies becoming less important because people are busy?
3. Do people prefer active hobbies or relaxing hobbies?
4. Should hobbies be useful, or is enjoyment enough?
5. How do hobbies affect mental health?

---

## 13. Culture and Traditions

1. Why is it important to keep traditions?
2. Do traditions change naturally over time?
3. How can modern life affect local culture?
4. Should young people learn more about traditional culture?
5. Can globalisation weaken local identity?

---

## 14. Communication

1. How important are communication skills in daily life?
2. Are face-to-face conversations better than online communication?
3. Why do some people find it difficult to express themselves clearly?
4. Should schools teach communication skills more directly?
5. Has technology improved communication or made it worse?

---

## 15. Change and the Future

1. Why do some people find change difficult?
2. Is change usually positive or negative?
3. Do young people accept change more easily than older people?
4. What areas of life will change most in the future?
5. How can people prepare better for change?

## 五、最后建议

题库最有效的练法不是“看很多题”，而是：

* 抽题
* 限时
* 录音
* 复盘

每次复盘只看 3 件事：

* 有没有直接回答问题
* 有没有展开而不是一句结束
* 有没有过度重复简单词

如果你后面继续推进，最自然的下一步就是把这份题库做成：

* `按周抽题版`
* `随机题卡版`
* `中英对照口语卡片版`

这样你的 IELTS 资料链就会比较完整。
